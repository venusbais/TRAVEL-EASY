Maharashtra-The Land Of Lord Ganesha




"Mumbai-THE CITY OF DREAMS"


Speckled with Victorian buildings, lofty skyscrapers, the glamour of Bollywood, pulsating nightlife and romantic beaches, the mega-city of Mumbai is a mecca for dreamers looking to make it big.





"Pune-CULTURAL CAPITAL OF MAHARASHTRA"


It has historic landmarks, hill forts, picnic spots and fabulous party places make it a favourite with the tourists for quick weekend sojourns





"Nashik-VINEYARD CAPITAL OF MAHARASHTRA"


On one hand a spiritually awakened tourist spot, while on the other it is considered the wine and grape capital of India





"Ratnagiri-HOME OF THE ALPHONSO MANGOES"


Surrounded by the lush hills of the Western Ghats, the sleepy hamlet of Ratnagiri offers the best of this Western Maharashtra’s charming landscape, cuisine, panoramic views, beaches and forests





"Alibaug-SCENIC COASTAL TOWN IN MAHARASHTRA"


Locally known as Alichi Bagh, Alibaug ia a popular beachside destination located in the konkan region of Maharashtra. Surrounded by the Arabian Sea, it’s sun soaked beaches, historical forts and picturesque picnic spots make it a perfect weekend getaway.





"Lonavala & Khandala-QUAINT HILL STATION IN MAHARASHTRA"


Just 4.6 kms apart from each other, Lonavala and khandala are home to dramatic landscapes, historical forts, ancient caves, charming sunsets, beautiful lakes and cascading waterfalls





"Mahabaleshwar-THE STRAWBERRY CAPITAL OF MAHARASHTRA"


Mahabaleshwar is the ideal hill station getaway from Mumbai with it’s mesmerizing waterfalls, old forts, historic temples and long stretches of vibrant strawberry plantations.
