Karnataka-Fusion Of Different Cultures




"Coorg-SCENIC HILL STATION"


Unwind in the lap of nature in Coorg where aromatic coffee and spice plantations, lush green hills, cascading waterfalls and a distinct old-world charm sooth the senses.





"Mysore-SCENIC ROYAL CITY"


The City of Palaces, Mysore revels in its vibrant history and rich arts and culture. It allures tourists with its grand palaces, majestic temples and landscaped gardens.





"Chikmagalur- LUSH GREEN HILL STATION"


A hill station at the foothills of the Mullayangiri peak in the Western Ghats in Karnataka, Chikmagalur entices with trekking trails, coffee plantations, rivers, waterfalls, temples and wildlife.





"Mangalore-SERENE PORT CITY"


With the azure Arabian Sea on one side and the lush Western Ghats on the other side, Mnagalore is a popular destination for a laid-back holiday.





"Gokarna-SCENIC TEMPLE TOWN "


Home to pristine beaches bordered by swaying palms and renowned Hindu shrines, Gokarna is an unspoilt temple town in Karnataka. It lies close to tourist desstinations like Goa, Mangalore and Hubli.
