Kerala—God’s Own Country




"Aleppey-NATURE LOVER’S PARADISE IN KERALA"


Also known as Alappuzha, Aleppey will take your breath away with its emerald green backwaters, palm fringed lakes and beautiful stretches of lush green fields in the heart of kerala.





"Munnar-EXOTIC TEA PARADISE IN KERALA"


The biggest tea plantation area in south india,Munnar is also home to several endangered species.It is a spectacular stretch of silent mountains and thick forests.





"Wayanad-GREEN PARADISE IN NORTH-EAST KERALA"


Located on the southern tip of Deccan plateau, Wayanad is home to clear lakes,dense jungles and deep valleys. This weekend getaway from Bengaluru is a paradise for trkkers.





"Varkala-A TRANQUIL COAST TOWN IN KERALA"


Flaunting one of the best cliff beaches in kerala, Varkala is a lesser known yet absolutely paradisiacal destination that houses forts, pilgrimage sites, springs, natural fisheries and more.





"Vagamon-IDYLIC HILL STATION IN KERALA"


Surrounded by lush green tea gardens, verdant pine forests and small waterfalls,Vagamon is home to three hills,namely, Thangal hill,Murugan hill and kurisumala. The hill station is perfect for adventure-seekers and nature-lovers.





"Thrissur-A CULTURAL HUB"


Known as the cultural capital of Kerala, Thrissur is a land of performing arts,festivals and religious sites. It is also home to waterfalls,beaches and dams.
